Breakfast

Meals

The talk is about working with ingredients from within the local 100km. That is something that the Ozio Rustic Restaurant try to adhere to. Not only for good, fresh, ingredients, but also to build a stronger community.

Desserts

Ever have one of those days? We’re pretty sure we have a dessert that can help. To ease your conscience, we use nothing but fresh fruit, drizzled with chocolate, caramel or fudge - with whip cream to top it off. Yes, you can share your dessert with a friend.

Coffee

Coffee people know that coffee is not just a warm, morning beverage - it’s more like a necessity, mush like air. We have a large selection for the coffee connoisseur. (We also take care of our tea lovers as well.)

Community

Sharing the experience, photos and swapping stories, there is no better way to enjoy your local community than over good food. Joining the community will earn you more than a simple Facebook like.
